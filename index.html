Technical Challenges in Study

    • Revocation should be taken into account
  2. To build a multi-factor authentication
    • BBS+ is chosen as the key factor technology
    • The non-key factor should no reveal more information than BBS+.

    • A credential is issued by multiple authorities.
    • It satisfies RP's predicate.
    • But some attributes are issued by RP's untrusted authority
